Music is our passion

The first world Festival was organized back in 1993 as a small gathering and has since evolved into one of Europe’s largest music festivals. Each year we strive to bring world some of the biggest names out there, and host over 1,000 shows across 60 stages, for 6 days straight.

29 June-6 July, full week pass £120

Festivals come from all over the world

There are more than 100 nationalities represented at Festival world, with people coming from as far away as Brazil or New Zealand. The peaceful gathering of different cultures for the common goal of music, celebration and entertainment is what Freedom Island is all about.

29 June-6 July, full week pass £120
